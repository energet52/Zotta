Champion-Challenger Testing

Your proven champion strategy makes every real decision while a challenger silently evaluates the same applications and records what it would have done. After enough volume, you see exactly where they agree, where they diverge, and which one performs better ‚Äî backed by actual default rates, approval volumes, and projected revenue impact, not guesswork.

  • Real decisions always made by your proven champion ‚Äî zero risk to your portfolio during testing
  • Challenger evaluates every application in shadow mode and records its would-be decisions
  • Side-by-side comparison: agreement rate, default rates, approval volumes, and projected revenue
  • One-click promotion when data proves the challenger wins ‚Äî or discard and try a new approach
  • Continuous, controlled experimentation that eliminates the risk of switching strategies blindly
  • Full audit trail of every test with start date, traffic volume, and outcome metrics
